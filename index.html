Doll-Me-Do: Find the killer doll movie for YOU.

New to the killer doll genre, or horror as a whole? Don’t know where to start? Or perhaps you’re a seasoned horror fiend looking for suggestions? Whoever you are, put that knife down and take a deep breath; Doll-Me-Do is here to help.

For each of the 10 criteria below, choose your preferred option. Once you’ve selected all 10 options hit the Generate Result button. Your perfect killer doll movie will appear, matching all of your selected criteria.
